    5. Market Perception: Cider is often marketed as a premium or craft beverage, and its price may reflect this positioning. Craft and artisanal ciders tend to command higher prices due to their perceived quality and niche appeal.

  10. ChatGPT said:

    SHU (Scoville Heat Units) is a measurement of the spiciness or heat of chili peppers and other spicy foods. It quantifies the concentration of capsaicin, the compound responsible for the heat sensation. The higher the SHU, the hotter the pepper.

    The scale was developed by Wilbur Scoville in 1912 and measures how much the chili extract needs to be diluted before its heat is no longer detectable by taste testers. For example:

    • Bell Pepper: 0 SHU (no heat)
    • Jalapeo: 2,500 - 8,000 SHU (mild to moderate heat)
    • Cayenne Pepper: 30,000 - 50,000 SHU (moderate heat)
    • Habanero: 100,000 - 350,000 SHU (very hot)
    • Scotch Bonnet: 100,000 - 350,000 SHU (very hot, similar to habanero)
    • Carolina Reaper: 1,400,000 - 2,200,000 SHU (extremely hot, one of the hottest peppers in the world)

    SHU is used to compare the heat intensity of various peppers, helping consumers and chefs choose the appropriate pepper for their needs based on desired spice levels.

    2. Use script {} for Complex Groovy Code
